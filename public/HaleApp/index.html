Objective
Create an app that not only gives a personal training assistance but also provides actionable tips based on the goals and activity history. All this with great adaptive visual and emphatic interface that will empower a student's daily life with inspiration, motivation and insight.
“Fitness apps of the future should focus on helping users learn to like physical activity for what it is, perhaps by offering more personalization options”. ready4s.com
A Story Behind the design
I’m not a fitness person, but I like to have a healthy life. So, find an app that helps tracking my exercises and also shows my progress in a simple way, it’s been a challenge.

After making a detailed study of the top fitness apps such as “8fit, Nike Run Club, Relive, Strava” , I realized that they are based on general training plans, but they were lacking to be focused on your personalized activities and routines under my interests. Besides, these apps provide very detailed data that might be irrelevant for the user, so I reckon that the challenge would also need to simplify the data that is provided and be simpler and relevant for the user. For instance, a user who loves running would be only interested in distance, calories and time, the rest of the information could seem unimportant for him/her. Moreover, if we reduce data we also reduce battery use. Thus, I made myself the following questions.

Is it really important for the user to have exhausted detailed real-data?

After I have proved with active and non-active fitness users I can say that all of us agree that we do not need too much information for our workouts, it actually distracts the user. On another note, the battery consumes faster when the app deals with exhausted data.


What could be better?
A machine learning that analyses and learns from user’s habits and routines. Every time that the user sets a goal and completes an activity, the app can learn from the data collected on the activity and recommend a new method to improve the exercises.

Defining problems and solutions
Problem
Too much analytic data across the experience that distracts the user.

Solution
Accurate data based on user’s activity and preference. This allows the user to have a personalized experience with info based on their own preference.

Problem
Battery Usage: A serious disadvantage of fitness apps is that they require a lot of energy to track the activity of users

Solution
A user interface with a dark pattern design to improve the battery usage and a contrasted night routine workout. In addition, it displays the info that the user really needs depending on the activity.

They have the potential room to grow up. However, they experience the same pain points about their motivation, as well as, their overexposed data information.

Personas I
Fitness freaks who exercise all day.

Personas II
People like me who want to be fit and healthy but need some motivation to go to the gym or exercise for the day.
